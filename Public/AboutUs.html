Our Story

Who We Are......

Erimu Company was established in 1992 as a Business venture to exploit the possibility of processing and manufacture of high quality wood products. Seven family members from the lineage of the Late Eridad Mukasa founded it. The Company was fully registered in 1995 as required by the Company Act of 1985, as ERIMU Company Limited. Two of the members of the Board are the active shareholders/ Directors and their respective positions.
